**Training Requirements**

**1. Prerequisites**

Before attending training, trainees must have:
    - Completed any required prerequisite training relevant to the topic 

    - Submitted proof of prior competency (certificates, assessments, or supervisor confirmation) 

    - Met any equipment or software access requirements (if applicable) 

The Training Coordinator must verify prerequisites before enrolment.

**2. Materials Required**

- Training slides, handouts, or written guidance 

- Checklist or rubric for competency assessment 

- Training attendance and feedback forms 

- Required resources for the topic (equipment, software, accounts, mock datasets, etc., depending on the training)
